ABOUT

I believe in making things that matter. As an interdisciplinary designer with experience in architecture, graphic design and interaction design, my passionate and creative attitude can be seen in both digital and physical products that are functional and enjoyable. In 2008, I completed my Master's in Architecture at the University of Minnesota, specializing in conceptual design and 3D modeling. In August of 2016, I'll be completing my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction + Design at the University of Washington. My diverse work experience includes 8 years as a designer at award-winning architecture firms, along with 2 years at a small tech company, where I was a lead interaction designer.
