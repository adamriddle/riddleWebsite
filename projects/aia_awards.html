AIA NATIONAL AWARDS

This was a design competition through the American Institute of Architects (AIA) for young architecture professionals to submit work they've participated in or created themselves. Out of all the submissions, the winning posters were displayed at the National AIA headquarters in Washington, DC. The two poster designs below are projects that I independently designed, produced and submitted. The reason is because I wanted to show my skill set as a singular focus and I also wanted to limit any influences or outside resources. These projects represent my ideas, my design skills, displayed on a national scale.
